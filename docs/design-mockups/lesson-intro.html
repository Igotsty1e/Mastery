Verbs Followed by -ing

10 exercises ~5 minutes Grammar
Use

Some verbs are followed directly by the -ing form, not by to + infinitive. In this lesson, focus on common verbs such as enjoy, avoid, consider, mind, suggest, keep and finish.

Form
verb + -ing
  • enjoy working
  • avoid making
  • suggest taking
  • mind waiting
  • keep complaining
  • finish writing
Important

After these verbs, do not use to + infinitive. Say I enjoy reading, not I enjoy to read.

Examples
  • I enjoy working with international clients.
  • She suggested taking a taxi because it was late.
  • Do you mind waiting a few minutes?
