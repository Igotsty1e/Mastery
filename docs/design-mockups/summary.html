Verbs Followed by -ing · B2

Strong work on -ing patterns. Watch out for sentences using mind and suggest — both want the -ing form, never to + infinitive.

Review your mistakes

2

"She suggested to take a taxi because it was late."

Answer She suggested taking a taxi because it was late.

After suggest, do not use to + infinitive when another verb follows directly. Use the -ing form: suggest taking.

"I don't mind to wait for a few minutes."

Answer I don't mind waiting for a few minutes.

Mind is followed by the -ing form, not to + infinitive. The correct pattern is mind waiting.
